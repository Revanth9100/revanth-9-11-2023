iQOO Neo 7

Overview

The iQOO Neo 7 is a powerful upper mid-range smartphone launched in India in October 2023. It boasts several impressive features, including:

  • **Processor:** A cutting-edge **Dimensity 8200** processor built on a **4nm** process, ensuring smooth performance and high energy efficiency.
  • **Display:** A large **6.78-inch** AMOLED display with a **120Hz** refresh rate for silky-smooth visuals.
  • **Camera:** A triple-lens camera system featuring a **64MP** main sensor with OIS, a 2MP depth sensor, and a 2MP macro sensor.
  • **Battery:** A massive **5000mAh** battery with support for **120W FlashCharge**, allowing you to charge 50% of the battery in just 10 minutes.

Additional Features

  • Motion control for enhanced gaming experience.
  • 90 FPS gaming capabilities.
  • Full coverage 3D cooling system for optimal thermal management.
  • In-display fingerprint sensor.
  • 1200Hz Instant Touch sampling rate for improved touch responsiveness.
